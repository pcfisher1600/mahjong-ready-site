Mahjong Ready — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Mahjong Ready is a single-user, offline learning app. It does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal data.

What we store on your device: your practice progress (mastery estimates), onboarding selection, the most recently completed game for replay, and any in-progress game you save. All of this is stored in the app's own sandboxed UserDefaults on your device. It is deleted when you delete the app.

What we do not do: we do not track you, we do not run analytics, we do not include advertising, we do not contact any server over the network, and we do not include any third-party SDKs. The app works fully offline by design.

Children: the app is appropriate for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
